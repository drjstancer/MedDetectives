# Production Deployment Verification

## Purpose

This verification process ensures the MedDetectives experience remains immersive, operationally stable, emotionally grounded, and faithful to the room-first investigation philosophy.

The goal is NOT technical perfection alone.

The goal is preserving:
- immersion,
- uncertainty,
- collaborative reasoning,
- emotional realism,
- and facilitator orchestration.

---

# Pre-Deployment Verification

## Room Integrity

Verify:
- evidence placement consistency,
- hidden artifact accessibility,
- timeline reconstruction usability,
- QR integration flow,
- environmental immersion,
- and collaborative movement space.

The room should never feel:
- instructional,
- classroom-oriented,
- or digitally dominant.

---

# Digital System Verification

## Participant Experience

Verify:
- QR scanning responsiveness,
- stage progression accuracy,
- escalation reveal timing,
- investigation feed stability,
- and mobile readability.

The interface should support the room rather than compete with it.

---

## Facilitator Experience

Verify:
- session activation controls,
- escalation triggers,
- timer functionality,
- environmental guidance systems,
- audio escalation recommendations,
- and debrief export visibility.

---

# Multi-Team Verification

Verify:
- simultaneous session stability,
- independent escalation pacing,
- contradiction tracking separation,
- and facilitator visibility across teams.

---

# Emotional & Narrative Verification

Verify:
- uncertainty remains productive,
- contradictions deepen interpretation,
- environmental escalation feels intentional,
- and the investigation maintains emotional realism.

The experience should never collapse into:
- puzzle solving alone,
- lecture facilitation,
- or educational software behavior.

---

# Mobile Verification

Verify:
- QR readability,
- evidence readability,
- button accessibility,
- timer visibility,
- and room usability under movement conditions.

Participants should remain physically engaged with the room.

---

# Final Deployment Questions

Before production deployment ask:

- Does the room still feel authoritative?
- Does uncertainty remain central?
- Does the facilitator feel like a narrative conductor?
- Does collaborative reasoning emerge naturally?
- Does the digital layer remain supportive rather than dominant?
- Does the experience still feel emotionally human?

If not, recalibrate before deployment.
