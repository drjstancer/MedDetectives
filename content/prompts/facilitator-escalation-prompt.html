Facilitator Escalation Clue Card Prompt

Create a facilitator-controlled escalation clue card for a hybrid educational clinical reasoning escape-room.

The card should:
-guide participants toward reinterpretation
-encourage collaborative discussion
-avoid giving direct answers
-reinforce uncertainty tolerance
-prompt evidence comparison
-feel facilitator-authored and educationally intentional

Include phrases like:
-“Which evidence felt most trustworthy initially?”
-“What assumptions may need revisiting?”
-“Does confidence always equal accuracy?”

Style:
-professionally printed facilitator clue card
-warm cinematic educational aesthetic
-minimalist modern academic design
-realistic paper materials
-immersive collaborative learning atmosphere
