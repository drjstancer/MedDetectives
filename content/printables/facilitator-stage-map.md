# Jordan Carter Case — Facilitator Stage Map

## Stage 1 — Scenario Activation
Participants:
- activate investigation
- establish team roles
- begin evidence exploration

Facilitator Focus:
- encourage collaborative communication
- avoid early interpretation guidance

---

## Stage 2 — Conflicting Narratives
Participants:
- discover inconsistent witness perspectives
- identify emotional context clues
- begin interpretation tension

Facilitator Focus:
- encourage evidence comparison
- normalize uncertainty
- redirect overconfidence

---

## Stage 3 — Evidence Reconciliation
Participants:
- compare timeline inconsistencies
- revisit assumptions
- synthesize contradictory evidence

Facilitator Focus:
- encourage iterative reasoning
- support collaborative synthesis
- avoid confirming interpretations

---

## Stage 4 — Final Synthesis
Participants:
- present best-supported interpretation
- justify reasoning
- reflect on evidence quality

Facilitator Focus:
- reinforce reasoning process over correctness
- transition into reflective debrief
