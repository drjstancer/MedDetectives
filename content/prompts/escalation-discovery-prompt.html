Escalation Discoveries Prompt

Create cinematic “escalation discovery” evidence artifacts for a facilitator-controlled educational reasoning experience. These discoveries should introduce tension and force reinterpretation of earlier assumptions.

Include:
-sealed envelopes
-late-stage observation notes
-contradictory recollections
-revised timelines
-hidden participant comments
-facilitator-controlled reveal materials
-subtle emotional tension
-evidence that changes interpretation but does not provide a final answer

Style:
-immersive educational thriller
-realistic paper materials
-cinematic lighting
-sophisticated academic simulation aesthetic
