# Reconnect Continuity Philosophy

## Core Principle

Participants should experience:
- investigation continuity,
- collaborative restoration,
- and immersive re-entry.

They should not feel like they are recovering software.

---

# Re-entry Rather Than Recovery

Reconnect experiences should emphasize:
- returning to the investigation,
- restoring collaborative interpretation,
- and maintaining emotional continuity.

Language should avoid:
- technical jargon,
- system-centric framing,
- and software-recovery terminology.

---

# The Room Remains Primary

The physical room remains the authoritative investigation environment.

Reconnect systems exist only to:
- preserve continuity,
- restore synchronization,
- and support facilitator orchestration.

---

# Appropriate Reconnect Messaging

Appropriate language includes:
- investigation continuity,
- collaborative restoration,
- re-entry into the investigation,
- and facilitator-guided pacing.

Avoid language focused on:
- applications,
- databases,
- systems,
- or technical recovery.

---

# Vision Alignment

Participants should feel:
- reconnected to the investigation,
- not reconnected to software.
