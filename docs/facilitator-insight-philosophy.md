# Facilitator Insight Philosophy

## Core Principle

Facilitator insights exist to support:
- pacing,
- synthesis,
- collaborative reasoning,
- and interpretive movement.

They do NOT exist to:
- automate facilitation,
- provide answers,
- or direct participant conclusions.

---

# Human-Centered Facilitation

Facilitators remain:
- conductors,
- observers,
- and moderators of collaborative meaning-making.

The system may recommend:
- pacing adjustments,
- contradiction releases,
- or synthesis prompts.

The facilitator determines:
- whether intervention is appropriate,
- when escalation should occur,
- and how ambiguity should be preserved.

---

# Anti-Drift Guardrail

Insights should never become:
- algorithmic gameplay guidance,
- participant scoring,
- automated correction,
- or deterministic progression systems.

The investigation remains:
- interpretive,
- uncertain,
- collaborative,
- and human.

---

# Vision Alignment

The platform supports:
- facilitator awareness,
- pacing continuity,
- and collaborative investigation.

The room remains the experience.
