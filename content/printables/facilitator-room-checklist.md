# Jordan Carter Case — Facilitator Room Checklist

## Physical Room Setup
- Arrange collaborative evidence board
- Place sticky notes and markers at synthesis station
- Position printed evidence artifacts in designated zones
- Place QR discovery sheets in hidden but accessible locations
- Prepare participant journals and timeline fragments
- Prepare facilitator clue envelopes

## Required Materials
- Evidence cards
- Witness observation sheets
- Timeline fragments
- QR sheets
- Access PIN slips
- Participant journal pages
- Tape or clips for evidence board
- Whiteboard markers
- Timer display device

## Facilitator Readiness
- Activate session before participants enter
- Verify synchronized countdown timer
- Review clue progression guidance
- Prepare reflection and debrief prompts

## Educational Facilitation Reminders
- Encourage collaborative interpretation
- Normalize uncertainty and evolving reasoning
- Redirect participants instead of giving answers
- Preserve productive struggle before offering clues
