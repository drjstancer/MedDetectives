# Jordan Carter Case — Facilitator Debrief Guide

## Debrief Goals
Participants should reflect on:
- collaborative reasoning
- uncertainty tolerance
- interpretation revision
- evidence synthesis
- communication under pressure

## Reflection Prompts
1. Which evidence initially shaped your assumptions?
2. What evidence forced your group to reconsider?
3. How did your team navigate disagreement?
4. Which clue changed your interpretation most significantly?
5. What parallels exist between this experience and clinical reasoning?

## Facilitator Guidance
- Normalize evolving interpretations.
- Reinforce that uncertainty is expected in complex reasoning environments.
- Emphasize evidence synthesis over finding a perfect answer.
- Encourage quieter participants to reflect on moments where they noticed overlooked information.

## Educational Framing
This experience was designed to strengthen:
- collaborative interpretation
- reasoning under uncertainty
- communication
- reflective synthesis
- and comfort with evolving evidence.
