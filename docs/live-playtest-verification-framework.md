# Live Playtest Verification Framework

## Purpose

This framework evaluates whether live MedDetectives sessions preserve the intended immersive investigation experience.

The goal is NOT merely confirming technical functionality.

The goal is verifying:
- uncertainty,
- emotional realism,
- collaborative reasoning,
- environmental immersion,
- and facilitator orchestration.

---

# Core Evaluation Questions

## Investigation Authenticity

During playtesting ask:

- Do participants physically investigate naturally?
- Do teams collaborate organically?
- Do contradictions destabilize certainty effectively?
- Does the room feel emotionally believable?
- Does the investigation remain human-centered?

---

# Drift Detection

Watch carefully for:
- excessive screen focus,
- worksheet behavior,
- facilitator over-teaching,
- puzzle-only engagement,
- lecture energy,
- or rigid answer-seeking.

If these emerge, recalibration is required.

---

# Escalation Verification

Verify:
- contradiction timing,
- environmental pacing,
- emotional escalation,
- collaborative tension,
- and interpretation instability.

Participants should revise assumptions repeatedly.

---

# Facilitator Verification

Verify facilitators:
- preserve ambiguity,
- avoid solving the investigation,
- modulate tension intentionally,
- intervene minimally,
- and sustain immersive pacing.

---

# Multi-Team Verification

Verify:
- simultaneous sessions remain manageable,
- escalation remains differentiated,
- facilitator attention remains balanced,
- and collaborative pacing remains stable.

---

# Post-Session Reflection

After each playtest document:
- strongest emotional moments,
- strongest collaborative moments,
- moments where immersion weakened,
- moments where digital systems became too dominant,
- and moments where uncertainty became especially productive.

---

# Final Verification Standard

The experience succeeds when participants leave discussing:
- the investigation,
- the uncertainty,
- the contradictions,
- the emotional complexity,
- and their evolving interpretations.

NOT the technology.
