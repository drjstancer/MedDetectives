Observation Evidence Cards Prompt

Create professional observation evidence cards for a collaborative educational reasoning experience. These cards should contain observational statements from participants and witnesses during a leadership event.

Include:
-emotionally nuanced observations
-uncertainty
-conflicting perceptions
-incomplete recollections
-realistic formatting
-educational simulation materials
-believable participant voice
-evidence requiring synthesis

Style:
-realistic printed cards
-cinematic tabletop photography
-educational immersion aesthetic
-warm realistic lighting
