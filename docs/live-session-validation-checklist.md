# Live Session Validation Checklist

## Purpose

This checklist validates whether real-world MedDetectives sessions preserve the intended immersive investigation experience during live deployment.

The goal is confirming:
- immersion,
- uncertainty,
- collaborative reasoning,
- emotional realism,
- and facilitator orchestration.

---

# Pre-Session Validation

Verify:
- room atmosphere feels immersive,
- evidence placement feels believable,
- QR assets scan reliably,
- escalation systems are operational,
- and facilitator dashboard controls are functioning.

---

# Live Investigation Validation

Observe whether participants:
- physically investigate,
- collaborate naturally,
- revise assumptions,
- debate interpretations,
- and revisit evidence organically.

---

# Drift Detection

Watch for:
- worksheet behavior,
- screen fixation,
- facilitator over-teaching,
- answer-race behavior,
- or excessive focus on technology.

If these emerge, recalibration is required.

---

# Emotional Validation

Verify:
- emotional tension escalates intentionally,
- contradictions deepen interpretation,
- and reflective moments emerge naturally.

Participants should experience uncertainty emotionally, not merely intellectually.

---

# Facilitator Validation

Verify facilitators:
- preserve ambiguity,
- sustain immersive pacing,
- redirect gently,
- and avoid solving the investigation.

---

# Final Success Standard

The session succeeds when participants leave discussing:
- the investigation,
- the contradictions,
- the emotional complexity,
- and the evolution of their interpretations.

Not the mechanics.
