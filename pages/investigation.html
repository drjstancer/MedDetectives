Active Investigation Briefing

Jordan Carter — Escalation Reconstruction

Jordan Carter, a second-year Biological Sciences student and highly involved campus leader, experienced a major emotional and physiological escalation event during a late-night collaborative planning session.

Multiple witnesses reported subtle behavioral shifts in the weeks leading up to the incident, but their interpretations conflict. Some described Jordan as focused and composed. Others noticed emotional withdrawal, exhaustion, perfectionism, and increasing frustration.

Your investigative team has been tasked with reconstructing the evolving timeline, analyzing contradictory observations, and determining what warning signs may have been overlooked.

This is not a criminal investigation. Your role is to collaboratively synthesize evidence, interpret uncertainty, and evaluate how different people understood the same events in different ways.
