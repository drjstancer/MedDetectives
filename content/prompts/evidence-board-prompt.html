Evidence Board Arrangement Prompt

Create a cinematic collaborative evidence board for an educational clinical reasoning escape-room called “MedDetectives: Jordan Carter Case.” The board should feel like undergraduate pre-med students are actively synthesizing evidence in real time.

Include:
- Timeline fragments pinned inconsistently
- Witness statement cards
- Sticky notes with handwritten hypotheses
- Contradictory observations connected with string
- Photos, printed journals, QR markers, and evidence tags
- Evolving collaborative interpretations
- Color-coded markers and annotations
- Areas where students revised earlier assumptions
- Uncertainty and ambiguity visually represented
- Realistic educational investigation atmosphere
- NOT police procedural style
- NOT crime investigation aesthetic
- Educational and collaborative tone
- Warm cinematic lighting
- Evidence synthesis rather than “solving a crime”
- Style: immersive academic thriller, educational investigation board, photorealistic, highly detailed
