Timeline Fragment Cards Prompt

Create printable timeline fragment cards for a collaborative educational investigation experience. The cards should represent fragmented and partially conflicting recollections of a student leadership event timeline.
Include:
-timestamps
-fragmented event descriptions
-incomplete sequencing
-slight contradictions
-professional educational formatting
-realistic printed strips/cards
-collaborative reconstruction feel
-cards designed to be physically rearranged by participants

Style:

-photorealistic printed paper
-cinematic tabletop arrangement
-immersive educational simulation materials
-realistic shadows and textures
