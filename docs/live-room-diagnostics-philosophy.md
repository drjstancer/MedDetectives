# Live Room Diagnostics Philosophy

## Core Principle

Diagnostics exist to preserve:
- investigation continuity,
- collaborative pacing,
- facilitator awareness,
- and immersive stability.

Diagnostics should remain operationally invisible to participants whenever possible.

---

# The Room Remains Primary

The room itself remains the authoritative investigation environment.

Diagnostics support facilitators by:
- identifying synchronization instability,
- highlighting continuity risks,
- and protecting collaborative movement.

The platform should never become the focus of participant attention.

---

# Appropriate Diagnostic Uses

Appropriate uses include:
- identifying desynchronization,
- recognizing continuity failures,
- preserving escalation pacing,
- and restoring collaborative stability.

Diagnostics should support:
- facilitator judgment,
- not facilitator replacement.

---

# Anti-Drift Guardrail

Diagnostics should never become:
- participant scoring systems,
- facilitator automation,
- rigid workflow enforcement,
- or algorithmic investigation control.

The investigation remains:
- collaborative,
- interpretive,
- emotionally layered,
- and human-centered.

---

# Vision Alignment

The technology layer exists only to support:
- continuity,
- synchronization,
- pacing,
- and facilitator orchestration.

The room remains the immersive experience.
