# Telemetry Philosophy

## Core Principle

Telemetry exists to support:
- facilitator awareness,
- pacing continuity,
- collaborative investigation,
- and operational refinement.

Telemetry does NOT exist to:
- grade participants,
- quantify human worth,
- automate interpretation,
- or reduce ambiguity.

---

# Human-Centered Investigation

The MedDetectives experience is:
- collaborative,
- interpretive,
- emotionally layered,
- and uncertain.

Telemetry should support facilitators in preserving:
- momentum,
- synthesis,
- and collaborative reasoning.

---

# Appropriate Telemetry Uses

Appropriate telemetry uses include:
- recognizing stagnation,
- identifying overlooked contradictions,
- supporting reconnect continuity,
- and preserving session flow.

Telemetry should remain operationally invisible to participants whenever possible.

---

# Anti-Drift Guardrail

Telemetry should never become:
- participant scoring,
- hidden grading,
- algorithmic facilitation,
- or predictive participant profiling.

The experience remains:
- human-centered,
- facilitator-led,
- and interpretively open.

---

# Vision Alignment

The room remains the immersive experience.

The technology layer exists only to support:
- pacing,
- continuity,
- synchronization,
- and facilitator orchestration.
