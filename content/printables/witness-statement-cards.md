# Jordan Carter Case — Witness Statement Cards

## Witness Statement A
"Jordan seemed calm before the leadership activity started. I remember everyone joking and talking normally."

Facilitator Note:
This statement should initially appear reliable.

---

## Witness Statement B
"Jordan looked distracted and overwhelmed earlier in the evening, but nobody really stopped to ask if they were okay."

Facilitator Note:
Encourage participants to compare emotional observations against timeline evidence.

---

## Witness Statement C
"I thought Jordan left the room before the event escalated, but now I’m not completely sure about the timing."

Facilitator Note:
This statement should increase collaborative uncertainty and timeline tension.
