# Jordan Carter Case — Room Layout Guide

## Investigation Zone A — Initial Evidence Table
Place:
- witness statement cards
- participant journals
- timeline fragments

Purpose:
Participants begin collaborative interpretation and identify conflicting perspectives.

---

## Investigation Zone B — QR Discovery Area
Place:
- QR discovery sheets
- hidden journal fragments
- facilitator-approved escalation discoveries

Purpose:
Encourage movement, environmental exploration, and evolving evidence synthesis.

---

## Investigation Zone C — Evidence Reconciliation Board
Place:
- sticky notes
- markers
- timeline reconstruction materials
- collaborative synthesis board

Purpose:
Participants compare interpretations and negotiate evidence meaning.

---

## Investigation Zone D — Restricted Observation Station
Place:
- sealed observation envelope
- access PIN artifacts
- contradictory evidence discovery

Purpose:
Introduce uncertainty tension and challenge earlier assumptions.

---

## Facilitator Positioning Guidance
Facilitators should remain:
- visible but non-dominant
- supportive without directing conclusions
- responsive to collaboration breakdowns
- attentive to participant reasoning quality

Avoid:
- confirming interpretations too early
- over-directing participant decisions
- interrupting productive struggle
