Hidden Journal Fragments Prompt

Create small hidden journal fragments designed for physical concealment in a hybrid educational escape-room. The fragments should look torn from a larger notebook.

Include:
-partial sentences
-emotional uncertainty
-fragmented thoughts
-incomplete reflections
-realistic torn paper edges
-handwritten notes
-subtle ambiguity
-clues requiring interpretation rather than direct answers

Style:
-photorealistic torn notebook paper
-cinematic lighting
-realistic handwriting
-educational immersive simulation aesthetic
