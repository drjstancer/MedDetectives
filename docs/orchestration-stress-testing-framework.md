# Orchestration Stress Testing Framework

## Purpose

This framework validates that the technology layer remains supportive, invisible, and resilient.

The objective is not software performance alone.

The objective is preservation of immersive investigation continuity.

---

# Test Scenario 1

## Facilitator Device Failure

Expected Outcome:
- room continues operating,
- physical evidence remains authoritative,
- facilitator restores continuity without disrupting immersion.

---

# Test Scenario 2

## Participant Reconnect

Expected Outcome:
- participant re-enters investigation,
- continuity is preserved,
- collaborative interpretation continues.

---

# Test Scenario 3

## Multi-Session Operation

Expected Outcome:
- facilitators maintain session separation,
- synchronization remains stable,
- escalation pacing remains independent.

---

# Test Scenario 4

## Synchronization Mismatch

Expected Outcome:
- mismatch detected,
- facilitator notified quietly,
- room continuity preserved.

---

# Test Scenario 5

## Telemetry Failure

Expected Outcome:
- investigation continues,
- facilitator relies on room observation,
- orchestration continuity remains intact.

---

# Success Metric

The room continues functioning even when technology does not.

That is the ultimate continuity test.
