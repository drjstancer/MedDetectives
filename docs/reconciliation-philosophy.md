# Reconciliation Philosophy

## Core Principle

Synchronization systems exist to preserve:
- investigation continuity,
- collaborative pacing,
- facilitator orchestration,
- and participant immersion.

The technology layer should quietly maintain continuity without becoming visible or dominant.

---

# Authoritative State Philosophy

Facilitator orchestration remains the authoritative investigation state.

This preserves:
- pacing integrity,
- escalation continuity,
- collaborative timing,
- and room-first facilitation.

Participants should not experience:
- competing realities,
- desynchronized escalation,
- or fragmented investigation timelines.

---

# Conflict Resolution Philosophy

Conflict resolution exists to:
- restore continuity,
- preserve pacing,
- and maintain collaborative immersion.

Conflict resolution should never:
- override human facilitation judgment,
- replace facilitator authority,
- or automate interpretive meaning-making.

---

# Anti-Drift Guardrail

Synchronization systems should never become:
- algorithmic gameplay engines,
- participant scoring systems,
- or deterministic progression mechanics.

The investigation remains:
- collaborative,
- interpretive,
- uncertain,
- and human-centered.

---

# Vision Alignment

The room remains the immersive authority.

The technology layer exists to support:
- continuity,
- synchronization,
- pacing,
- and facilitator orchestration.
