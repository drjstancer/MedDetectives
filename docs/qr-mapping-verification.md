# QR Mapping Verification

## Purpose

This verification framework ensures QR progression pathways reinforce immersive investigation flow and physical-room authority.

QR pathways should:
- support movement,
- deepen investigation,
- intensify interpretation,
- and preserve collaborative discovery.

QR pathways should NOT:
- create scavenger-hunt energy,
- dominate participant attention,
- or reduce the room to technology interaction.

---

# Verification Areas

## Environmental Integration

Verify QR markers:
- blend naturally into evidence,
- feel contextually believable,
- and support narrative realism.

---

## Investigation Flow

Verify QR pathways:
- reinforce collaborative reasoning,
- support evidence synthesis,
- and deepen interpretive uncertainty.

---

## Escalation Mapping

Verify escalation QR reveals:
- emerge intentionally,
- destabilize assumptions,
- alter interpretation,
- and intensify emotional realism.

---

# Participant Behavior Verification

Watch for:
- QR hunting behavior,
- technology fixation,
- isolated screen interaction,
- or disengagement from physical evidence.

If these emerge, recalibration is required.

---

# Facilitator Verification

Verify facilitators:
- redirect participants toward collaborative synthesis,
- preserve physical-room authority,
- and avoid turning QR interaction into gameplay focus.

---

# Final Verification Standard

Participants should remember:
- the investigation,
- the uncertainty,
- the contradictions,
- and the emotional complexity.

Not the QR system.
