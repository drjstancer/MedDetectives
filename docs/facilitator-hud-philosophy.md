# Facilitator HUD Philosophy

## Core Principle

The facilitator HUD exists to support:
- orchestration awareness,
- pacing continuity,
- collaborative stability,
- and facilitator confidence.

The HUD should never become the center of the investigation experience.

---

# The Facilitator Remains Primary

The HUD supports:
- awareness,
- continuity,
- synchronization,
- and pacing.

The facilitator remains:
- the conductor,
- the moderator,
- and the guardian of collaborative interpretation.

The HUD should never:
- dictate facilitation,
- automate escalation,
- or reduce ambiguity.

---

# Appropriate HUD Uses

Appropriate uses include:
- monitoring continuity,
- recognizing synchronization instability,
- identifying collaboration risks,
- and preserving investigation pacing.

The HUD should support:
- facilitator judgment,
- not facilitator replacement.

---

# Anti-Drift Guardrail

The HUD should never become:
- a control room simulator,
- participant surveillance infrastructure,
- rigid workflow enforcement,
- or deterministic investigation management.

The investigation remains:
- collaborative,
- facilitator-led,
- interpretive,
- emotionally layered,
- and human-centered.

---

# Vision Alignment

The room remains the immersive authority.

The technology layer exists only to support:
- continuity,
- synchronization,
- pacing,
- and facilitator orchestration.
