Participant Journal Prompt

Create realistic handwritten participant journal pages for an immersive educational escape-room focused on collaborative clinical reasoning. The journal entries should belong to “Jordan Carter,” an overwhelmed student participant preparing for a leadership event.
Include:
-handwritten reflections
-stress masked by professionalism
-emotional ambiguity
-incomplete thoughts
-crossed-out phrases
-realistic notebook pages
-subtle hints but no explicit answers
-believable undergraduate voice
-emotionally human tone
-references to pressure, exhaustion, expectations, and uncertainty

Avoid:
-melodrama
-horror
-obvious diagnosis language
-explicit conclusions

Style:  
-realistic journal photography
-warm desk lighting
-notebook textures
-cinematic realism
-subtle emotional atmosphere
