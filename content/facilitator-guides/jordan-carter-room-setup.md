# Jordan Carter Case — Room Setup Guide

## Experience Goal
Create an immersive collaborative investigation environment that encourages evidence interpretation, discussion, and reasoning under time pressure.

---

# Room Zones

## Orientation Area
Materials:
- Welcome briefing envelope
- Session activation instructions
- Participant journal fragment

Purpose:
Introduce the investigation context and establish collaborative expectations.

---

## Evidence Board Zone
Materials:
- Printed evidence cards
- Sticky notes
- String/yarn for evidence mapping
- Whiteboard markers

Purpose:
Support collaborative synthesis and evolving interpretation.

---

## Timeline Station
Materials:
- Timeline fragments
- Contradictory observation notes
- QR markers

Purpose:
Encourage sequencing discussion and uncertainty navigation.

---

## Facilitator Materials
Prepare:
- 3 clue envelopes
- Reflection prompts
- Stage transition notes
- Debrief questions

---

# Facilitation Notes
- Avoid confirming participant interpretations too early.
- Encourage groups to explain reasoning aloud.
- Normalize disagreement and evolving interpretations.
- Use clues to redirect thinking, not provide solutions.
