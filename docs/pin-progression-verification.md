# PIN Progression Verification

## Purpose

This verification process ensures PIN-based progression systems reinforce immersive investigation pacing rather than feeling artificially gamified.

PIN systems should:
- support discovery,
- reinforce collaborative investigation,
- and validate physical-room reasoning.

PIN systems should NOT:
- dominate gameplay,
- feel puzzle-centric,
- or replace collaborative interpretation.

---

# Verification Areas

## Physical Evidence Alignment

Verify PIN progression:
- emerges naturally from evidence,
- rewards investigation rather than guessing,
- and reinforces collaborative synthesis.

---

## Escalation Timing

Verify PIN unlocks:
- occur at intentional narrative moments,
- support uncertainty progression,
- and deepen interpretation rather than simply opening content.

---

## Participant Behavior Verification

Watch for:
- random guessing,
- code-hunting behavior,
- excessive screen fixation,
- or puzzle-first engagement.

If these dominate, recalibration is required.

---

# Facilitator Verification

Verify facilitators:
- redirect participants toward evidence,
- preserve immersion during failed attempts,
- and avoid over-assisting.

---

# Final Verification Standard

Participants should experience PIN progression as:
- investigative validation,
- collaborative discovery,
- and narrative escalation.

Not as arbitrary puzzle mechanics.
