# Final Narrative Pacing Polish

## Purpose

This framework refines the emotional, interpretive, and collaborative pacing of the MedDetectives experience.

The goal is preserving:
- escalating uncertainty,
- evolving interpretation,
- emotional realism,
- collaborative instability,
- and reflective synthesis.

The experience should feel intentionally paced rather than mechanically sequenced.

---

# Pacing Philosophy

Escalation should feel:
- organic,
- emotionally believable,
- psychologically destabilizing,
- and collaboratively disruptive.

Participants should repeatedly:
- revise assumptions,
- revisit evidence,
- renegotiate interpretations,
- and experience uncertainty shifts.

---

# Critical Pacing Areas

## Early Investigation

Verify:
- curiosity emerges naturally,
- the room feels exploratory,
- and participants begin collaborative synthesis quickly.

Avoid excessive exposition.

---

## Mid-Investigation Instability

Verify:
- contradictions intensify uncertainty,
- confidence destabilizes,
- and emotional complexity deepens.

Participants should begin questioning earlier assumptions.

---

## Escalation Phase

Verify:
- emotional tension increases intentionally,
- collaborative disagreement becomes productive,
- and interpretation instability peaks.

Avoid collapsing ambiguity too early.

---

## Reflective Resolution

Verify:
- participants shift from answer-seeking toward reasoning reflection,
- collaborative synthesis feels emotionally grounded,
- and unresolved tension remains meaningful.

The experience should not end with simplistic certainty.

---

# Drift Detection During Pacing

Watch for:
- puzzle-rush behavior,
- facilitator over-guidance,
- answer fixation,
- or emotional flattening.

If these emerge, pacing recalibration is required.

---

# Final Verification Standard

The experience succeeds when participants remember:
- evolving uncertainty,
- emotional complexity,
- collaborative interpretation,
- and reasoning instability.

Not merely clue progression.
